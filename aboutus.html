ABOUT US


OUR CLIENTS

We work with homeowners who are planning to live in their home long term, usually for ten or more years. They live in a home that is too small, or the layout or functionality of the house doesn’t work anymore. Or maybe the finishes are falling apart. Often they’ve bought the perfect starter home. But as the family grew they needed more space, or to use the space in a different way. They have considered moving, but staying in their current home makes more sense. They love their neighbourhood, and would prefer not to move. They are looking for help with both design and construction, and would prefer to work with a single firm which will be responsible for both design and construction.

THE COMPANY

For the past 15 years, homeowners have called us for solutions, and to design and build their renovation, house addition or custom home. Along the way, we've built a team which includes an in-house designer, project managers, and carpenters. We've been nominated for several awards, and have won two best renovation awards. We service the Greater Toronto Area and areas to north-west.

WE ARE RELIABLE AND HIGHLY SKILLED
